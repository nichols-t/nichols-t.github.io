mini infinity pipe

september 2024

i wanted to play around with infinity mirrors so i got some plastic rolls of half-mirror and mirror to play with.

i used a toilet paper tube which i cut roughly in half, then some acrylic i had (don't remember where that was from).

to add some impressions of "pipe" i wrapped some aluminum wire around the inside and outside and put pencil parts in the inside.

then i jammed a hole in the middle for a micro LED that i got for a semi-successful silmarils project.
