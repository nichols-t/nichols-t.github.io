pajama shirt

march 2026

i made a set of pajamas over thanksgiving 2025 at my parents' but i improvised the shirt and it was kinda bad.

so i bought a hippari pattern because that looked like it would be an interesting shirt to use as pajamas.

i got blue linen and also i had bought a sewing machine for my new apartment because why would i want to not have a tool lying around?

i made a muslin that came out decent so i just did the real one as soon as the blue arrived. after i was done i figured the fancy leaf stitch would look good around the edges with yellow thread so i did that on a slower speed.
